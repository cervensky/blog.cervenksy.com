The Penguin's Reign

You're currently (probably) in Windows City. You can have many great adventures in there, like playing a game, editing photos and videos, writing a letter on your favorite word processing program or doing stuff on the Internet. And that's great and fun, but sometimes you see a person walking into a subway station and waiting for a train. That person usually takes the train to a place called "The Penguin's Reign". You wonder how it is like there, outside of Windows City. Thus, one day you decide to also take that train and take a look at that foreign place. Once you arrive, you only see darkness. How is that person able to navigate and what is he even doing in there all the time?
You take the train back home, but you now are even more curious about that weird place and the person regularly going there. You ask around a bit and people throw words at you like "web server", "network security", "moonbuggy", "android". You have no idea what they're talking about and now went to this self-made website of that Cervensky dude, which will make things clear, hopefully.
